Scale Shot

"The user attacks by shooting scales two to five times in a row. This move boosts the user's Speed stat but lowers its Defense stat."

Effect

Inflicts regular damage two to five times in a row, raising the user's Speed and lowering the user's Defense by one stage each upon last hit.
